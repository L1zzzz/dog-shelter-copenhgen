How to donate ?

As an organization that relies solely on volunteer work, we are heavily dependent on our sponsors and donations from both companies and individuals, as well as private initiatives (flea markets, handicrafts and organizing events for Greek dogs).

If you want to help us in the fight for the fate of dogs, please contact us via email : mail@graeskehunde.dk.
