WHO ARE WE?

We are Græske Hunde.
A non-profit volunteer-based organisation helping out Greek street dogs get better lives.
If you’ve ever been to Greece, maybe you’ve met a dog on the street just wandering around. So did we. But we didn’t stop there.
We’ve dedicated over 20 years of our lives to create a better environment for dogs on the streets of Greece,
by sending dogs to families in Denmark, and by working directly on the streets of Greece.
We have no expenses for administration, website or anything else, because it is our principle that everyone
is unpaid because every penny must go straight to help the animals.
